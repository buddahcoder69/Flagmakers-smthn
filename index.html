Welcome to The Flagmakers!

The Flagmakers is a friend group of custom flag designers and remakers using the number one tool for making flags: Google Slides!

Google Slides may seem like a bad contender for uhh, flag designing, but trust me, it's what we've started with and will never leave from. As a presentation software, even though it may not seem like it, google slides has all of the shapes needed for accurate flagmaking. Coincidentally enough, now popular youtuber Dr. Zye, made two (soon to be three) videos about flagmaking in Powerpoint, Google Slides' counterpart.
